Our Story

Born from one too many sleepless nights.

We started Nocturne after years of settling for uncomfortable, cheap eye masks that let in light, pressed on our eyes, and fell off the moment we moved. We knew there had to be a better way.

Two years and 47 prototypes later, we created the sleep mask we always wanted — one that treats sleep as the luxury ritual it deserves to be. Premium materials. Thoughtful engineering. Beautiful packaging.
